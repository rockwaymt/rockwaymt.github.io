About us

Providing pain relief, injury prevention and recovery, performance enhancement, anxiety and stress reduction.

Welcome to Aestheticart.ca

We offer a large variety of non-invasive, but effective facial treatments for acne, rosacea and hyperpigmentation, chemical peels, micro-needling, LED Light Therapy, Oxygen Therapy and many more. We will go above and beyond in order to provide you with an unparalleled and rewarding experience.

Our deepest desire is to contribute to your self-improvement by enhancing your skin condition through our exquisite treatments, followed and sustained by a correct day-to-day routine at home. We also provide practical advice and we offer our knowledge of the latest techniques and procedures for face and skin care, in order to enrich the self-care knowledge that everyone needs.

We bring simple, but elaborate solutions and we address your needs with personalized treatments and dedicated products for each specific situation. Our main goal is to help you to augment your skin to a flawless condition. For this, we use only non-invasive treatment methods and the most effective skincare products available on the market.

While treatments are provided with a high level of professionalism, our focus is on safeguarding the health and well-being of our valued clients. This is achieved through scrupulous attention given to safe work practices, and an outstanding level of hygiene.

Covid Info

Providing Wellness Support in a trying time

Here is how we are handling Covid safety:

You will be sent covid-19 pre-screening questions to review prior to your appointment. You must be symptom-free in order to attend.

Please come as close to your appointment time as possible. When you arrive, please wait in your car and call 519-722-8774.

Everyone entering the clinic is required to wear a mask. Your temperature will be checked once you have entered the building.

Please come to your appointment alone if possible, unless you require assistance. We cannot allow guests to wait in the clinic.

Please use provided hand sanitizer when entering and exiting the clinic.

Cleaning of the clinic has been increased to ensure proper disinfecting.
